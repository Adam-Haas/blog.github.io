Hello world 👋

My name is Adam Haas - welcome to my blog.

I'm a Senior Backend Software Engineer, and I've been writing software professionally since 2018. Outside of work, I've been going through something of a personal reinvention since November 2024 (more on that in future posts).

Lately I've found myself drawn to bigger questions than the ones I solve at work. What does it actually mean to live? Is social media quietly eroding our ability to think, feel, and connect? Do we need to do hard things to feel anything at all?

This blog is where I'll explore those questions — not with any claim to have the answers, but because putting thoughts into the world feels like a good way to figure them out. If any of it resonates with you, I'd love to hear from you.
